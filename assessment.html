Business IT & Security Self‑Evaluation

A rapid assessment designed to help business owners and practice managers evaluate operational resilience, cybersecurity readiness, and potential compliance risks.

Company Information

1. Business Continuity & Data Resiliency

1.1 Our backups are isolated from our primary environment and protected from ransomware.



1.2 We have performed a restore test within the last six months.



1.3 We have automated backup Internet/failover connectivity.

2. Digital Perimeter & Endpoint Security

2.1 MFA is enforced on email, cloud services, and remote access.



2.2 We use Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR/XDR).



2.3 Employees do not have local administrator rights.

3. Operational Overhead & Lifecycle Management

3.1 Operating systems and security updates are automatically deployed.



3.2 All company devices use supported operating systems.



3.3 New employee onboarding takes less than 24 hours.

4. Regulatory Compliance & Accountability

4.1 We can produce security, patching, and training records quickly.



4.2 Guest Wi‑Fi is isolated from internal systems.



4.3 Technology planning reviews occur at least twice per year.



Scoring Guide

10–12 Yes Answers → Resilient

Your environment is highly proactive and well protected.

6–9 Yes Answers → Degraded Risk

Several gaps exist that could create operational disruption.

0–5 Yes Answers → Critical Exposure

Significant cybersecurity and business continuity improvements are recommended.
